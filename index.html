Hello


My name is Joanna Han. I'm a recent grad from MIT with a B.S. in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science. I'm 21 years old and prefer to live life by going with the flow everyday.

I used to swim competitively between the ages of 4 and 19. I quit swimming my sophomore year of college, but now I like to say that I just took a little 2 year break from it. I started back up in the new year of 2018, and now I enjoy swimming recreationally. You know the term runner's high? Swimmer's high pretty much brings out that same feeling of euphoria.

I'm a huge fan of music as well. I used to play the piano, violin, and trumpet. Recently, I've been fascinated with music production and am currently self-learning. I'm a fan of most genres, specifically anything under the umbrella of EDM (Tropical House, Deep House, Trap, Chillstep, etc.). I listen to music pretty much 24/7 - there's just so many wonders that music can do, ranging from being able to pump you up for something big or just help you relax and wind down after a long day.

Since I was a kid, I've always wanted to explore different places. After graduating from college, I have taken an interest in making those dreams a reality. I'm definitely more comfortable in large cities, but there's something innately serene about hiking up mountains while admiring the landscape that really makes me want to explore what's out there.
